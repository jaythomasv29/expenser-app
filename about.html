The inspiration for creating an expenser application came from the fact that most people do not budget effectively and our world is suffering from severe inflation. I myself am still trying to improve on this. If we are more mindful and aware of where all of our money goes then we might be cautious about spending habits. We live in an age where people have 4+ subscriptions to things monthly, and they are just lurking in the background waiting to be collected from our bank accounts.
